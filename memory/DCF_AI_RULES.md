## Missing Data Recovery (NEVER LEAVE BLANK)
| Metric | Formula | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| ROE | Net Income / Total Equity × 100 | ₹45B/₹300B = 15% |
| ROCE | EBIT / (Total Assets - Current Liabilities) × 100 | ₹80B/(₹500B-₹100B) = 20% |
| P/E | Current Price / EPS (TTM) | ₹1,800/₹60 = 30x |
| EPS | Net Income / Shares Outstanding | — |

## PEG Rule (Critical Valuation)
- **PEG = PE / (Revenue Growth × 100)**
- If PEG < 0.5: Company deeply undervalued relative to growth
- **Rule:** TGR MUST be ≥ 5% for PEG < 0.5 companies
- Example: P/E=12x, Growth=30% → PEG=0.4 → Use TGR=5.5-6%

## Multiple Expansion Scenario
When current P/E is LOW but ROCE is HIGH:
| Condition | Adjustment |
|-----------|------------|
| P/E < 15x + ROCE > 30% | Lower discount rate 0.5-1% |
| Quality not priced in | Use TGR at upper range (5% vs 3%) |

This captures re-rating potential when market recognizes quality.
